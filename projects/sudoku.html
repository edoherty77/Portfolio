Sudoku

A sudoku puzzle is a 1 player board game where the numbers 1-9 must appear only once in any given column, row, and 9x9 sqaure. In my app, players can choose up to 4 levels of difficulty, which each give an appropriate amount of time to finish the puzzle. Once a player chooses a difficulty, the "start" button will become enabled, the the player can press start. Once clicked, a random puzzle will appear and the time will start. If the player pauses the game, the timer will stop and the puzzle will disappear, and a message will be provided. When the player resumes the game and the puzzle is once again rendered, the player can click on an empty square to select it, and then choose any of the numbers from the bottom of the screen. If the number picked is correct, the number will be added and the square will be become disabled. If the number is wrong, the number will appear in red and all other squares will become disabled. Once the player clicks the undo button, the red number will disappear and a mistake will be added to your tally of mistakes. You're allowed 3! If a player is stuck, they can choose to select the hint button.
