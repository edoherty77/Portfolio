Travel Book

Previous Next

With Travel Book, users can easily keep track of the places they have visted, the photos they have taken, and even the miles they have travled. With the help of Google Maps and Places, and implementing MongoDB, Express, React Native, and Node, users can not only check-in to their current location with just the touch of a button, but also add photos. And since everything for that cool trip you went on is in one place, it's easier to brag to your friends.
