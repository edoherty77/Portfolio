Let's Chat

Previous Next

As a GA student, I frequently used Slack as a means of communication and education. Our main channel was populated with useful information, but since it was so active, finding the information you needed became difficult, even with Slack's search feature. Let's Chat was created to alleviate this issue by requiring users to select a tag relevant to the topic they are posting. Using Express and MongoDB, I implemented a search feature where users can sort posts by selecting their desired tag.
